SDG 4 – Quality Education

1. Overview

This SDG focuses on ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education for all learners.

2. Purpose

The project connects students to tutors based on learning needs and academic goals.

3. How It Helps

Filtering by subject, level, and tutor experience increases learning efficiency.

4. Impact

Improves academic performance and gives personalized support to every student.

SDG 9 – Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

1. Overview

Focuses on building digital innovation and technological growth in society.

2. Purpose

The project represents a modern educational technology system.

3. How It Helps

Uses digital forms, filters, and matching algorithms to enhance education services.

4. Impact

Promotes tech-based learning solutions that improve accessibility and efficiency.

SDG 10 – Reduced Inequalities

1. Overview

Ensures equal opportunities for all individuals irrespective of background.

2. Purpose

The platform treats all students equally and provides unbiased tutor suggestions.

3. How It Helps

The matching process ignores money, caste, gender, or location—only skill matters.

4. Impact

Reduces educational inequality and brings equal learning opportunities for everyone.

SDG 8 – Decent Work & Economic Growth

1. Overview

Promotes productive employment and economic opportunity for all.

2. Purpose

The system gives tutors access to work, helping them earn and grow financially.

3. How It Helps

Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced levels help students select tutors efficiently.

4. Impact

Enhances tutors' income potential and supports a sustainable education-based economy.

SDG 17 – Partnerships for the Goals

1. Overview

Encourages collaboration to solve global and local challenges.

2. Purpose

This system builds cooperation between students, tutors, and educational communities.

3. How It Helps

Connects learners to experts, forming strong educational networks.

4. Impact

Strengthens partnerships that contribute to high-quality learning and support systems.
